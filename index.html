About Camellia
 
  • 1992: Born in Asahikawa, Hokkaido.
  • 2003: Started composing on his mother's PC at the age of 10.
  • 2011: Won the Grand Prix in the "Drum Material Utilization Contest 2010 Rock Kit Category / Techno Kit Category" at "Cleo Fuga".

  • 2013: Received the highest award at "KAC (KONAMI Arcade Championship) 2013 Original Music Contest".

  • After appearing as a DJ at "Nico Nico Chokaigi", he was featured on Fuji TV.

  • Arranged for "Atasikittomugen Looper" and "Tsukatte Portfolio" (in the name of Masaya Oyasu) at Shiritsu Ebisu Junior High School.
